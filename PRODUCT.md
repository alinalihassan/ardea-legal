# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Individuals and small businesses in the Netherlands who need legal help without the overhead of a large firm. They arrive with a concrete matter to resolve, limited patience for jargon, and a need to trust that Berend can handle volume-oriented legal work reliably. They are not looking for a niche specialist in one domain; they want a capable, accessible lawyer who can take on work efficiently.

## Product Purpose

A personal brand site for a relatively new freelance lawyer building a volume practice in the Netherlands. The site converts visitors into hire-me conversations by making Berend's scope, approach, and availability clear. Success means a visitor understands what kinds of legal work Berend takes on, feels confident in his competence despite being early in his independent practice, and knows exactly how to get in touch.

## Brand Personality

Confident, minimal, expert. Professional without corporate stiffness: trustworthy and direct, not intimidating. Restrained and intentional — clarity and competence over decoration. Calm authority that suits legal work while staying approachable for individuals and small businesses.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages: cream backgrounds, card grids, gradient accents, hero metrics
- Over-designed or flashy interfaces with gratuitous motion
- Big-firm corporate tone: marble lobby aesthetics, impenetrable jargon, prestige signaling
- Obvious templates or "AI-made" visual grammar (section eyebrows, numbered markers, identical icon-card grids)
- Specialist-niche positioning that suggests Berend only handles one domain

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity before credentials** — say plainly what legal work Berend handles and how to start; reduce friction for volume intake
2. **Trust without pretense** — professional and credible for a newer independent practice, honest about scope, no inflated big-firm posturing
3. **Restraint is confidence** — remove before adding; hierarchy and plain language do the selling
4. **One clear path to hire** — contact or inquiry is obvious from any scroll depth
5. **Craft over template** — the site should feel authored and human, not assembled from a legal-marketing pattern library

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA baseline. Semantic HTML, keyboard navigation, visible focus states, sufficient color contrast. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for any animation.

## Language

Dutch primary. Copy and UI in Dutch.
